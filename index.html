Exeter Railway Band

about us

Founded on the 15th of October 1944 by a group of Railway workers Exeter Railway Band has continued to rehearse on Tuesday evenings, going from strength to strength, proudly accepting the role of standard bearer for Brass music in the Exeter area.

Throughout the year we hold regular concerts and feature at various events in and around the City of Exeter. Recently we have began getting involved with the contesting scene and are currently a third section band who are trying to improve all the time.

We are a friendly inclusive band open to all. If you wish to play with us then please do not hesitate to contact us. Also, if you wish to hire us for your event, fete, wedding or wish to hold a concert alongside us then just let us know!
